Certainly! Here are some delicious appetizer options that are great for potlucks:

Spinach and Artichoke Dip:

A creamy and cheesy dip made with spinach and artichokes. Serve it with tortilla chips or sliced baguette.
Stuffed Mushrooms:

Mushrooms stuffed with a flavorful mixture of breadcrumbs, herbs, and cheese.
Caprese Skewers:

Cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves threaded onto skewers and drizzled with balsamic glaze.
Deviled Eggs:

Hard-boiled eggs filled with a creamy mixture of yolks, mayonnaise, mustard, and various seasonings.
Bruschetta:

Toasted baguette slices topped with a mixture of diced tomatoes, garlic, basil, and balsamic glaze.
Cheese and Charcuterie Board:

Assorted cheeses, cured meats, crackers, and some fruits like grapes or figs for a sophisticated option.
Guacamole with Tortilla Chips:

A classic dip made with ripe avocados, tomatoes, onions, cilantro, lime juice, and served with tortilla chips.
Chicken Wings:

Baked or grilled chicken wings with various sauces like buffalo, barbecue, or teriyaki.
Shrimp Cocktail:

Chilled shrimp served with a tangy cocktail sauce.
Hummus with Veggie Sticks:

Smooth and creamy chickpea dip served with an assortment of fresh vegetable sticks like carrots, cucumbers, and bell peppers.
Mini Quiches:

Bite-sized quiches with various fillings such as spinach and feta, bacon and cheese, or mushroom and Swiss.
Cucumber Bites:

Sliced cucumber topped with cream cheese, smoked salmon, and dill.
Sausage Rolls:

Puff pastry filled with seasoned sausage, baked until golden brown.
Fruit Salsa with Cinnamon Chips:

Fresh fruit salsa made with diced fruits like strawberries, kiwi, and mango, served with homemade cinnamon-sugar tortilla chips.
Stuffed Jalapeños:

Jalapeño peppers filled with cream cheese and wrapped in bacon.
Remember to consider any dietary restrictions or preferences of the potluck guests when choosing your appetizer. Enjoy the potluck!
